  • Follow-up: pigs

 Endemics follow-up claim for pigs. If the farmer is coming into the service from the old world, they'll need to confirm the test result of their review.

Farmer
  • Follow-up: beef cattle, positive result
  • Follow-up: beef cattle, negative result
  • Follow-up: beef cattle, positive result (returning old world user)
  • Follow-up: beef cattle, negative result (returning old world user)
 Endemics follow-up claim for beef cattle. If the farmer is coming into the service from the old world, they'll need to confirm the test result of their review.

If their review result was positive, they cannot complete their claim without confirming a PI hunt was completed.

If it was negative, they can claim for a PI hunt if it was recommended and undertaken by the vet.
