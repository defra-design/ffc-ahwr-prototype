Guidance

Farmers: how to claim funding to improve animal health and welfare

Find out how to claim for animal health and welfare reviews and endemic disease follow-ups.


From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Rural Payments Agency

Applies to England

Contents

  1. When to claim
  2. What information you’ll need to provide
  3. Claim funding
  4. Get help with your claim
{% include "includes/print-page.html" %}

When to claim

You can claim funding after the vet has given you your summary for an animal health and welfare review or endemic disease follow-up.

You can make claims until:

  • 19 September 2027.

Your review claim must be approved before you claim for the resulting follow-up.You must have followed the guidance on timing of reviews and follow-ups.

! Warning Endemic disease follow-up is not available for dairy cattle yet. This part of the service will be available soon. 

Information you’ll need to provide

When you claim you’ll need to give details from the vet’s summary.

Find out what needs to be included in the summary for a review.

Find out what needs to be included in the summary for a follow-up.

Claim funding to improve animal health and welfare.

Get help with your claim

Contact the Rural Payments Agency and give your agreement number if you:

  • cannot claim online
  • have a question about your claim
