Important

This updated service is for farmers who:

  • are new to the scheme

  • already have an agreement in the old schemes

    • Claim funding for a livestock health and welfare review or follow-up

    Use this service to claim funding for a vet to visit your farm to improve the health and welfare of your livestock and help reduce diseases.

    If you’re eligible, you can claim for:

    • a livestock health and welfare review
    • a follow-up

    If you already have an agreement in the old scheme

    If you have claimed for your last review, start now to sign an agreement for the new scheme.

    If you have not claimed for your last review, start now to either:

    • claim for your last review
    • withdraw your old agreement

    Claiming takes about 5 minutes.

    Before you start

    You’ll need your Rural Payments service customer reference number (CRN) and password to sign in.

    You must be eligible to claim. Read the guidance on how to claim funding before you use this service.

    Get help with your claim

    If you cannot claim online or have a question about your claim, contact the Rural Payments Agency.

    You will need to give your agreement number.

    • Email: ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk

    • Telephone: 03000 200 301
    • Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm, except bank holidays
