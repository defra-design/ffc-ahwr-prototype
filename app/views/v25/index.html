Claim journey: annual health and welfare review

User Journey Description

Farmer
  • First review claim
 The first claim a farmer makes through the service. They must select a species which they'll claim for throughout the life of the service.

Farmer
  • Second review claim:
    • Beef cattle
    • Dairy cattle
    • Pigs
    • Sheep
 This journey simulates a subsequent review claim, where the species being claimed for was defined in the first review claim.
