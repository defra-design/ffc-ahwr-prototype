The business has no eligible county parish holding (CPH) registered to it

You cannot apply for an annual health and welfare review for this business as it does not have an eligible CPH registered to it.

To be eligible a CPH must be registered in England.

The CPH cannot be:

  • an 8000 number used for abattoirs, pet food manufacturers, racing pigeon establishments and showgrounds
  • a 9000 number used for poultry keepers

If you think you do have an eligible CPH, contact the Rural Payments Agency (RPA) to update your records.

  • Rural Payments Agency
  • Telephone: 03000 200 301
  • Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm except public holidays
  • ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk

You can also apply for another business that has an eligible CPH.
