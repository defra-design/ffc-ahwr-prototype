The Annual Health and Welfare Review

Version 1.1

Vet Visits Sprint 7

User Journey Description

Farmer
  • Apply
  • Starting point of the service
  • The farmer is sent to a guidance page providing an overview of what to expect from the review. From there, a link passes the user onto the service start page. The start button initialises the application process. Farmers enter into an agreement with Defra and arrange their own vet visit.

Vet
  • Review
  • Data entry on review
  • The vet or vet practice enters data about who did the review, followed by the requested details about data collected during the farm visit.

Farmer
  • Claim
  • Review of vet's report
  • The farmer gets an email to say the vet has completed their part. Following the link in the email, they can review the report and submit their claim. Some users will be asked to upload evidence as well. Defra will pay the funding if data shows farmer's eligibility.

Internal staff
  • Back office
  • The internal application review system
  • RPA staff will be able to use this system to look up applications. It will feature the progression status and details entered by users.
Note: on "check your email" screens, use the email@address.com link to continue the journey via magic link emails.
