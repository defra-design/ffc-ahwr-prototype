Guidance

Recommended laboratories to carry out a Worming Treatment Check test in sheep

Published 20 April 2023

Applies to England

Contents

  1. APHA Carmarthen Veterinary Investigation Centre
  2. Biobest Laboratories
  3. Techion UK (FECPAK)
{% include "includes/print-page.html" %}

The Rural Payments Agency (RPA) recommends that vets send samples to a laboratory on this list.

These laboratories follow the SCOPS testing guidance for the review and meet one or more of the following specifications:

  • ISO/IEC 17025 accredited
  • UKAS accredited (UK accredited) for a worm egg count
  • part of the Animal and Plant Health Agency (APHA) VetQAS worm eggs count proficiency scheme and has provided results defined by the z-score as satisfactory

A farmer will not be eligible for funding unless samples are tested at a laboratory that meets at least one of these specifications.

If you have a question about laboratories used for review testing, or want to be added to this list, contact the RPA by email or phone.
