We recommend you arrange an endemic disease follow-up by following these steps

  1. Read the guidance about timing of endemic disease follow-ups and arrange a follow-up at the advised time. You will need to give them the agreement number.
  2. Read the guidance about what happens at an endemic disease follow-up. The guidance explains what you must ask the vet to do and what’s optional.
  3.  Ask the vet to read the guidance on endemic disease follow-ups.
  4. After the vet has completed the follow-up, you can claim your funding. Read the guidance on how to claim funding for an endemic disease follow-up.

If you do not want to have a funded follow-up, you can arrange your next review

Follow-ups help prevent the spread of disease in your livestock. If you choose not to have one, you can arrange your next livestock health and welfare review at the advised time.
