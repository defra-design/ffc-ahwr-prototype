Important

This updated service is for farmers who:

  • are new to the service

  • already have an agreement in the old service

    • Get funding to improve animal health and welfare

    Use this service to apply for funding for a vet to visit your farm to:

    • improve the health and welfare of your livestock
    • help reduce diseases

    If you’re eligible, you can apply for funding for:

    • animal health and welfare reviews
    • endemic disease follow-ups

    If you already have an agreement in the old scheme

    If you have claimed for your last review, start now to sign an agreement for the new service.

    If you have not claimed for your last review, start now to either:

    • claim for your last review
    • withdraw your old agreement

    Then you can sign an agreement for the new service.

    Applying takes around 5 minutes.

    Start now

    Before you start

    You’ll need your Rural Payments service customer reference number (CRN) and password to sign in.

    You must be eligible to apply for funding to improve animal health and welfare.

    Get help with your application

    If you cannot apply online or have a question about your application contact the Rural Payments Agency by email or phone.

    • Email: ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk

    • Telephone: 03000 200 301
    • Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm, except bank holidays
