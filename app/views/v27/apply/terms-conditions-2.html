Funding to improve animal health and welfare: Terms & Conditions 2024

Animal health and welfare agreement holders must follow these terms and conditions if they applied for an agreement after 4 February 2024.


From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Rural Payments Agency

Applies to England

Details

You must comply with these terms and conditions if you applied for an Animal health and welfare review agreement on or after 4 February 2024:

When you carry out a review or follow-up, you should:

  • get an animal health and welfare review
  • get a follow-up for endemics if required
  • use a vet summary for your claim

A farmer will not be eligible for funding unless you do both of these things.

Previous terms and conditions include the following:

  • Up to and including 6 February 2023: Annual health and welfare review of livestock - terms and conditions
  • 7 February 2023 to 13 June 2023: Annual health and welfare review of livestock - terms and conditions
