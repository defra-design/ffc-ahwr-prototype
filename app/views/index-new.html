About this service

The Animal Health and Welfare Pathway (the Pathway) was launched in 2023.

The Pathway supports continual improvement in farm animal health and welfare. It is a partnership with farmers, vets, the wider industry and the supply chain.

Pathway is a critical part of the farming reforms set out in the Agricultural Transition Plan, delivering benefits for animal health and welfare, farm productivity, food security, public health, UK trade and the environment.

Journeys in this prototype
Journey About this journey
Guidance Farmers and vets learn about the service with a step-by-step guide to animal health and welfare reviews and endemic disease follow-ups.
Apply The first interaction with the service. Farmers enter the service and agree to the terms and conditions.
Claim Once a vet has completed a review or follow-up, a farmer enters the detail of the visit and test results to make a funding claim.
Back office The internal application review system RPA staff use to look up claims and agreements.
