Apply for an annual health and welfare review of your livestock

Use this service to apply for an annual health and welfare review with your vet.

Applying takes around 5 minutes.

Start now

Before you start

You'll need the business email address associated with your Rural Payments Agency (RPA) account.

We'll send you an email that includes a link to apply for a review. This link will expire in 15 minutes.

You must be eligible to apply for an annual health and welfare review. Read the guidance on getting a funded annual health and welfare review of your livestock before you use this service.

Get help with your application

If you cannot apply online or have a question about your application, contact the Rural Payments Agency.

  • Rural Payments Agency
  • Telephone: 03000 200 301
  • Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm (except public holidays)
  • ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk
