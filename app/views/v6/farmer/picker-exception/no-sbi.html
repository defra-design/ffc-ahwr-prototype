There is no eligible business you can apply for

There is no business registered to your rural payments account that is eligible to apply for an annual health and welfare review.

To be eligible, a business must have:

  • a single business identifier (SBI) linked to a county parish holding (CPH) number registered in England
  • a CPH number that can be permanent or temporary but not an 8000 or 9000 number

What happens next

You can:

  • update your Rural Payments account if the details are incorrect and apply again
  • contact the Rural Payments Agency if you still have questions

Have your customer reference number with you when you call or include it in your email.

  • Rural Payments Agency
  • Telephone: 03000 200 301
  • Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm (except public holidays)
  • ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk
