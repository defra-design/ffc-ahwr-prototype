Claim funding for an annual health and welfare review

Use this service to claim funding for an annual health and welfare review.

Claiming takes about 10 minutes.

You’ll get your payment within 28 days of claiming. The payment will be sent to your Rural Payments account.

{{ govukButton({ text: "Start now", href: "email", isStartButton: true }) }}

Before you start

You’ll need:

  • the email address you applied with
  • vet's Royal College of Veterinary Surgeons (RCVS) number
  • the name of the vet practice
  • date of the last visit to the farm for the review
  • endemic disease or condition test results

Get help with your application

If you cannot claim online or have a question about your claim, contact the Rural Payments Agency.

If you have your reference number, have it with you when you call or include it in your email.

  • Telephone: 01900 508 622
  • Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm (except public holidays)
  • ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk
