How to claim funding to improve the health and welfare of your livestock

When to claim

You can claim funding after the vet has visited your farm to do a livestock health and welfare review or endemic disease follow-up. You’ll need a written report and review summary from the vet.

You can claim for reviews and follow-ups until:

  • [date] for cattle
  • [date] for pigs
  • [date] for sheep

You cannot claim for a follow-up until you’ve claimed for the review that happened before it. You must have followed the guidance on timing of visits.

Information you’ll need to provide

When you claim you’ll need to give details of the visit and testing from the vet’s summary.

Get help with your claim

Contact the Rural Payments Agency (RPA) if you cannot claim online or have a question about your claim. Have your agreement number ready if you have one.
