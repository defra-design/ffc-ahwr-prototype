Claim funding to improve livestock health and welfare

Use this service to claim for funding for a vet to visit your farm to:

  • improve the health and welfare of your livestock
  • help reduce diseases

If you’re eligible, you can claim funding for up to 3 of both:

  • livestock health and welfare reviews
  • endemic disease follow-ups

Before you start

You’ll need:

  • your Rural Payments service customer reference number (CRN) and password to sign in
  • the review summary the vet gave you to complete this application

You need to have completed a biosecurity assessment if you are claiming for an endemic disease follow-up for

  • dairy cattle
  • beef cattle
  • pigs

Get help with your claim

If you cannot claim online or have a question about your claim, contact the Rural Payments Agency.

You will need to give your agreement number.

  • Email: ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk

  • Telephone: 03000 200 301
  • Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm, except bank holidays
