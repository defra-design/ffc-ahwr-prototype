Timing of reviews and follow-ups

Reviews

You must:

  • do a review before you do the resulting follow-up
  • leave at least 10 months between your reviews

Follow-ups

You must:

  • leave no more than 10 months between a review and the resulting follow-up
  • leave at least 10 months between your follow-ups

Claims

A review claim must be approved before you claim for the resulting follow-up.
