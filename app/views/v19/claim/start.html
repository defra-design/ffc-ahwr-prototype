Claim funding to improve the health and welfare of your livestock

Use this service to claim funding for a vet to visit your farm to improve the health and welfare of your livestock and help reduce diseases.

If you’re eligible, you can claim for:

  • a livestock health and welfare review
  • a livestock disease-focused visit

    • Claiming takes about 5 minutes.

    Before you start

    You’ll need your Rural Payments service customer reference number (CRN) and password to sign in.

    You must be eligible to claim. Read the guidance on how to claim funding before you use this service.

    Get help with your claim

    If you cannot claim online or have a question about your claim, contact the Rural Payments Agency by email or phone.

    If you have your agreement number, have it with you when you call or include it in your email.

    • Email: ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk

    • Telephone: 03000 200 301
    • Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm, except bank holidays
