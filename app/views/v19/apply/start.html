Apply for funding for the annual health and welfare and follow-up reviews

Use this service to apply for funding for a vet to visit your farm to improve the health and welfare of your livestock and help reduce diseases.

If you’re eligible, you can apply for:

  • a livestock health and welfare review only
  • both a livestock health and welfare review and endemic disease-focused visit for the same species

Applying takes around 5 minutes.

Start now

Before you start

You’ll need your Rural Payments service customer reference number (CRN) and password to sign in.

You must be eligible to apply. Read the guidance on how to apply for funding before you use this service.

Get help with your application

If you cannot apply online or have a question about your application contact the Rural Payments Agency by email or phone.

  • Email: ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk

  • Telephone: 03000 200 301
  • Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm, except bank holidays
