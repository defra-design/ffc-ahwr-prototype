Recommended laboratories to test for bovine viral diarrhoea (BVD) in cattle

Published 20 April 2023

Applies to England

The Rural Payments Agency (RPA) recommends that vets send samples to a laboratory on this list.

These laboratories meet one or more of the following specifications:

  • ISO/IEC 17025 accredited
  • UKAS accredited (UK accredited) for BVD testing

A farmer will not be eligible for funding unless samples are tested at a laboratory that meets at least one of these specifications.

If you have a question about laboratories used for review testing, or want to be added to this list, contact the RPA by email or phone.
