Get funding to improve animal health and welfare

Cattle, sheep and pig farmers can get funding for a vet to test animals for diseases and advise on improving animal welfare and productivity.

From: Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs and Rural Payments Agency

This service provides funding for a vet, or team chosen by a vet, to visit a farm and help to improve animal welfare and productivity.

There are 2 parts to the service. The first part is the review which will help assess the health and condition of your livestock. The second part is the follow-up, which builds on a review to identify endemic diseases.

A review and follow-up will help:

  • reduce endemic diseases and conditions
  • increase animal productivity
  • improve animal welfare
