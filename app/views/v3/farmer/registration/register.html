Register your interest in a health and welfare review of your livestock

We need some information to check you’re eligible to register. It is important the information you provide is accurate and up to date.

To register, you’ll need:

  • your customer reference number (CRN)
  • the single business identifier (SBI) number of the business you want to apply for the review
  • the email associated to your Rural Payments account

If you need to update your SBI or email address you’ll need to sign in to sign in to the Rural Payments service before you use this service.

Problems with your CRN

If you’ve lost or forgotten your CRN, contact the Rural Payments Agency.

  • Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm (except public holidays)
